Onlylocalfood.com - we don't sell or deliver local food...


We show you who does!



Our platform shows you the farms and producers that have products available, including where and how to get them, and let's you order online. It's a farmers' market on the web, done right.

Feed your family.

1. Products and Schedules

Producers load their products onto OLF and schedule deliveries and pickups. Everything you need to know is added - what products are for sale, prices, and (this is the special bit) how, when, and where you can get your order.

2. Search and Order

Our search page shows you all the products that are available in your area. You can see who's selling and the delivery and pickup options. Add what you want to your cart, and instead of paying when you check out, you simply select how to get your order based on the producer's options.

3. Delivery, Pickup, and Payment

At the date and time you chose, you and the producer get together (or maybe they come to you!). You get the great local food you ordered and the producer gets paid directly. It's exactly like a farmers' market, just easier.
