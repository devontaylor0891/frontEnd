How it Works

Products and Schedules are Added

Producers load their products onto OLF and schedule deliveries and pickups. Everything you need to know is added - what products are for sale, prices, and (this is the special bit) how, when, and where you can get your order.

You Search and Order

Our search page shows you all the products that are available in your area. You see who's selling and the delivery or pickup options. Add what you want to your cart and select how to get your order based on the producer's options when you check out.

Get and Pay for Your Order

At the date and time you chose, you and the producer get together (or maybe they come to you!). You get the great local food you ordered and the producer gets paid directly. It's exactly like a farmers' market, just easier.

Why do it this way? And other questions...

So, why?

Short answer - because local food is too hard to find.

Local food is everywhere, but that's part of the problem. You can find it on Facebook or Twitter, in the newspaper or on the poster board at the post office, and not least of all, at the farmers' market. But maybe you don't want to do all that legwork - watching for Facebook posts or checking at the post office. Maybe you're at work when the farmers' market is open, if your community has one at all.

Onlylocalfood.com was created to bring producers and consumers of local food together in one online space.

What's the idea behind OLF?

OLF prides itself on not breaking the traditional local food buying process, only making it easier. We believe that in doing so, we can encourage more people to spend their money with local producers. And money that is spent in your community tends to stay there, benefiting everyone!
