Onlylocalfood.com is an online farmers’ market.


We give producers of local food an online storefront to showcase their goods. And we help consumers like you see exactly what’s out there, who’s selling, and how to get it. We’re working to make the local food market easy and efficient – all while keeping more of your dollars at home, wherever that home may be.

So, how does it work?

OLF does local food differently. It may seem complex at first, but at the heart of it, there are 3 simple steps:

Visit our Search page

  • We use your location to show you what products are available to order.
  • Filter those results to find just what you want.
  • Change the results view to see a listing of producers, or a calendar of pickups and deliveries.

Add items and checkout

  • Select products and add them to your cart.
  • When you checkout, choose how and when to get your order.
  • Add your details and confirm your order.

Get your goods!

  • At your chosen date and time, get together with the producer.
  • Pay them directly and get your order.
  • Gasp at how easy that was!

FAQ's and How To's

First, some basics...
As a consumer, why should I join?

There are several benefits to joining Onlylocalfood.com:

  • Discover - You can find new producers and products!
  • Save time - Your time is valuable, and you shouldn't need to hunt around looking for local food. OLF aims to be the one-stop online shop for you. And with our location notifications, you don't even have to come to the site anymore - sign up for them here and OLF will let you know when local food is coming to your area!
  • Pre-orders - Wouldn’t it be nice to go to a farmers’ market or roadside pickup with the products you want already ordered? So no matter what time you make it there, your order will be waiting. Don't miss out on the good stuff again!
  • Easy ordering - Rather than a long back-and-forth message string with a producer, you can now find all the information you need to order online with confidence. When prices, quantities, descriptions, and schedules are all transparently presented, you don't have to worry about any unexpected changes or charges.
  • Plan ahead - With the calendar in our search and each individual producer's calendar, you can look ahead to see what's coming and when.
Is OLF the retailer or distributor?

Nope. We are simply a platform that connects you to producers. We don’t want to be a middleman adding markup to products, and that helps us keep your cost down.

Producers operate their business as they do currently. But by adding their products and schedule to OLF, we can handle the marketing and order-taking.

What makes OLF so different from other online food delivery services?

Virtually every other local food website out there is a retailer/distributor. They buy the food from producers, and sell to consumers themselves. This adds a lot of cost to the transaction which can mean that producers get paid less, or you pay more. We want to keep the costs to you lower by helping you buy directly from the producer. They are already doing deliveries and attending markets, we just making finding and ordering easier for you.

What's available? What if there’s nothing for sale in my area?

OLF is producer-driven, meaning that we don’t control or manipulate what is available, when it's available, or where it's available. Availability will depend on what producers choose to sell, what’s in season, and where they want to sell. If you don't want to miss out when products do become available, sign up to our location notifications here. Then you don't have to keep checking back. We'll send you an email when deliveries and pickups are scheduled in the locations you select.

Costs and Services
How much does it cost, then?
  • As a consumer, using the site costs you nothing! You pay the producer directly for your order and we charge them a small fee after the order has been completed.
Are there delivery fees?

There could be. Door-to-door deliveries could have a fee attached to them, if the producer chooses. And that fee may apply to all orders, or it may be waived when a certain order value is reached. All this information will be available to see in the schedule details do you'll be aware before you order.

How to Use OLF
How do I sign up?

We've made signing up easy and safe by using a third-party service that is one of the best in the business - Auth0. Simply choose 'Sign In/Register' in at the top of any page, and use your email, or Google or Facebook account to create a basic account with us. Follow the instructions and you'll be set up in no time!

How do I search?

The most important part of your search starts with where you are. After all, you don’t really care what food is being delivered 3 hours away from you, do you? This is about local food.

A note about privacy: We don’t track you. We use the location you provide only to tailor your search results. That’s it. No bull.

Our search finds all the deliveries and pickups scheduled within a given radius of your location. It then shows you the products and producers associated with those results, and even the details about the deliveries and pickups themselves. This gives you all the information you need.

Looking specifically for veggies? Filter for produce and see the Products View. Want to order from just one producer that you already know? See the results from the Producers View. Or are you only available on Saturdays? Our Calendar View will show you what’s happening when.

How do I order?

Now that you’ve seen what’s on offer, you can start building your order. It’s just like ordering anything else online – start adding things to your cart! There is one special difference: because OLF is an online farmers’ market, that means when you order products from different producers, you are really creating multiple shopping carts. What does this mean? Simply check out one cart, then go back to your cart and checkout the next one.

Once you’ve added one item to your cart, you might want to check out the rest of the products from that producer by visiting their store. They’ve probably got more great products you’ll love!

After your cart is full, review your order. When you’ve confirmed you have what you want, continue to the checkout page. This is where OLF diverges from traditional online shopping once again: Your products won’t be shipped, so you’ll have to choose how and when to get your order based on the options the producer has set up. You can see what those options are by selecting a location.

After making the appropriate selection, fill out any extra details like a comment or special instructions for the producer, or your address if you chose delivery. Then hit ‘Confirm’ and you’re done! We send your order on to the Producer. Once they review and accept your order, you’ll get a confirmation email.

When will I get my order?

When you get your order is also chosen at checkout time based on the producer’s schedule. We’d suggest that you make a note of the date and time in your calendar as you check out.

I want to buy from multiple producers, how do I do that?

If you chose products from more than one producer, your cart will be split up into multiple carts. You will need to proceed through the checkout process for each cart.

What are the different schedule types that producers use?

Currently, OLF uses four types of schedules: Door-to-door delivery, market pickup, on-farm pickup, and other pickups.

Door-to-door Delivery - This is fairly self-explanatory, but you should note a couple of things: You will be required to provide an address when you order. If the producer is restricting their delivery in any way (eg. they are only delivering to the south west corner of the city), then it should be indicated that in the description. Producers also have the option to add a delivery fee, as well as the option to add it to every order, or to orders that fall below a certain dollar value.

Market Pickup - Again, pretty easy to understand. If a producer is taking pre-orders for a farmers' market, this schedule type they'll use.

On-farm Pickup - This option is for farmgate sales. Producers can set up their own schedule so you know exactly when you can show up to pick up your order. It probably wouldn't hurt to include some information in your comments at checkout - like when you expect to be there.

Other Pickup - This option will cover anything else. If the producer is going to drop off orders at another location (eg. a friend's store), or if they send meat to the butcher, this will be the option. The details in the description will be helpful to you to better explain how the pick up will work.

Can you ship my order?

We currently don’t offer the option for producers to ship products. This may become an option in the future.

What’s a location notification? And how do I create one?

You can make your search for local food a whole lot easier with our Location Notifications. Simply add one or more locations to your profile and as deliveries and pickups are scheduled near those locations, we'll send you an email telling you so! We'll do the searching for you.

You can add Location Notifications from your account dashboard. Or, you can simply head straight here to do it. While there, use the search to look up a community. Once you find it, click 'Add' to add it to your list. And don't forget to click 'Save' when you're done.

Managing Orders
What payment methods can I use with each producer?

As each producer runs their own business as they see fit, we don't control what payment methods they use. We do recommend that producers include the acceptable payment methods in their schedule descriptions.

Can I modify my order after I have checked out?

Your order goes through a few stages. When you first create it, it is marked ‘Pending’. Once the producer has reviewed it, they will mark it ‘Accepted’. If you need to make changes, they can only be made while it is ‘Pending’, before being accepted by the producer. If you need to order more products, you will have to create a new, separate order.

Account Management
How do I change/reset my password?

The third-party service we use for user/account management and security handles password resets. Get to the Auth0 widget by clicking 'Sign In/Register' at the top of any page. Near the bottom of the widget, you'll see 'Don't remember your password?'. Click that and follow the instructions.

How do I edit my account information?

In your Account Dashboard, you will find a tab named ‘My Account’. By clicking the ‘edit’ button you can update your information. Remember to click ‘Save’ when you are done!

Can I download a spreadsheet of my orders?

You sure can! You can download any of the tables in your Account Dashboard. Simply click the ‘Export’ button located beside the table’s title. A CSV file will be downloaded. You can then open that in Excel, or any other table-based software, and begin to manipulate the data.

Other Questions
What area is served? What exactly is local? Where does the food come from?

The beauty of an online space for local food is that the area served is whatever area a producer wants to serve. Producers could potentially come from anywhere. You get to decide how ‘local’ you want local to be. Our aim is simply to make things as local as possible.

Is the food organic?

OLF doesn’t restrict the types of food available on the site. Some producers will offer organic products and some will not. The information should be easy to find in the Producer Page or product information pages.

Why isn’t the producer's contact information on their page/storefront?

This goes back to our goal of trying to reduce the back-and-forth messaging that can come with selling food direct. You will have all the information they need to make a purchase decision right on the site itself, so we’ve made the decision to keep communication options limited. If you don’t agree with this, then we’d like to know more. You can send us a note and even ask us to call you back here.

How can I contact a producer?

Currently we are not including producer contact information on the site. All the information you need to create an order should be on OLF already. If you need more information from a producer, you can let us know and we can pass your information on to them. Simply use our contact form here.

I’m still not sure about this, is there someone I can talk to at OLF?

If you need more information, we’d be happy to help. You can use our contact form here. If you’d like a call back, please include your phone number and the best time to contact you and we’ll do our best to get back to you in a timely manner.
